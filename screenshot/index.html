Free Online Screenshot Beautifier

ScreenSnap transforms plain screenshots into polished, share-ready visuals in seconds. Drop your image, pick a gradient, and export — no sign-up, no uploads to external servers, and completely free. Everything runs directly in your browser for maximum privacy.

How to beautify screenshots online

Getting started is simple: drag and drop any PNG, JPG, or WebP image onto the canvas, or paste directly from your clipboard with Ctrl+V. ScreenSnap instantly applies a tasteful gradient background, rounded corners, and a soft shadow so your screenshot looks polished right away. Fine-tune the padding, corner radius, shadow intensity, and pick from over 30 gradient presets — including mesh gradients and glassmorphism-inspired looks that are trending in 2026. When you're happy, hit Download PNG or Copy to clipboard and share it anywhere.

Features

ScreenSnap packs powerful features into a zero-dependency tool: 30+ gradient backgrounds (linear, mesh, and glassmorphism), device frames for iPhone, MacBook, and Browser mockups, one-click export presets sized for Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Product Hunt, plus an optional "Made with Goosekit" watermark. Unlike cloud-based screenshot tools, ScreenSnap never uploads your images anywhere — your data stays on your machine. It's built as a single HTML file with zero dependencies, so it loads instantly and works offline.
