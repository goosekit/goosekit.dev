The product wedge

Refund Radar is not another analytics dashboard. It is a churn doctor for small products that need action, not charts.

Money events

Refunds, cancellations, failed payments, plan downgrades, and refund-window risk.

Reason capture

Simple cancellation/refund forms that ask why, then group answers into useful buckets.

Retention actions

Weekly fixes: FAQ changes, onboarding emails, winback copy, pricing objections, and product gaps.

A weekly report that tells you what to fix.
