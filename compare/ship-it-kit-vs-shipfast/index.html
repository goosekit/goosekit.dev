Which one should you pick?

Choose ShipFast if you want the full platform — email, SEO, analytics, and a larger community ecosystem, and you are comfortable with a bigger starter to onboard into.

Choose Ship It Kit if you want auth + billing done cleanly without inheriting a larger framework, and you want a smaller repo that AI coding tools (Cursor, Claude, Copilot) can navigate and edit quickly.

Ship It Kit is specifically for founders at the stage where signup, checkout, and the billing plumbing are slowing down the actual product work — not yet a full SaaS OS.
