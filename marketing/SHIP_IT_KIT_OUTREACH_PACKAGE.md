## Who to reach out to

Priority order:
1. **Builders you know personally** — friends, ex-colleagues, people in indie/dev communities
2. **People who've interacted with Goosekit** — Discord members, Twitter followers who engaged with dev tools posts
3. **Dev community contacts** — people in French indie hacker circles, your Twitter mentions from the Goosekit launch

---

## What to say to warm them up

- Frame as "I built this for myself, thought it might be useful for you"
- Offer to give feedback on their project if they're building something
- Ask them to share if they know someone who's building a SaaS right now

---

## Social proof to use (after first sale)

Once you have 1 buyer, update the outreach to:
> "Already got first buyers — happy to share what they're saying if you want to check the testimonials on the page."

---

## Measuring success

If you send to 20 warm contacts and get 2-3 responses + 1 sale, that's the first euro AND a testimonial to amplify IH/PH distribution.
