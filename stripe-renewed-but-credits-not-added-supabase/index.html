Stripe renewal credit drift

Stripe renewed, but Supabase credits were not added?

This is the recurring-revenue version of “paid but blocked”: Stripe has a successful renewal, yet the customer does not receive the monthly quota, credits, or entitlement window they paid for.

Fast incident triage

Send the renewal event, invoice ID, subscription ID, current Supabase credit row, and the intended monthly grant rule. Do not send secrets.

Request setup/debug help Monitor this monthly Build a reconciliation query

Likely failure

The handler processes invoice.paid or customer.subscription.updated, but the monthly grant is not idempotent, not keyed by period, or blocked by Supabase permissions.

Minimum invariant

For each active subscription period, exactly one grant exists for the customer/workspace/price. Retries must not double-grant, and missed retries must be visible.
