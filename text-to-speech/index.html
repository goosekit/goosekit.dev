Text to Speech

Paste or type text below and listen to it spoken aloud — works entirely in your browser.

How it works

This tool uses the Web Speech API built into modern browsers — your text never leaves your device. Choose from all voices installed on your system, tweak speed, pitch, and volume, then press Play.

Works best in Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Firefox supports basic voices. On mobile, tap Play to begin.
