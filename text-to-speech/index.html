Text to Speech

Paste or type text below and listen to it spoken aloud — works entirely in your browser.

Trying to pick the most natural voice for your text…

How it works

This tool uses the Web Speech API built into modern browsers, so your text stays on your device. Goosekit now auto-detects the text language and tries to pick the most natural matching voice available on your system.

Voice quality still depends on the voices installed in your browser or OS. Chrome, Edge, and Safari usually sound best. If a voice sounds odd, switch language or choose another voice manually.
