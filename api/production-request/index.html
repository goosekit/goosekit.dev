What happens next

Useful packets get reviewed for a narrow production pilot: endpoint, monthly volume, commercial context, failure mode, support boundary, and whether paid access makes sense.

  • Best fit: internal tools, backend jobs, QA/CI scripts, agents, or product workflows already blocked by the free tier.
  • Pricing is confirmed only after the packet is specific enough to support honestly.
  • Not a fit: bulk scraping, secrets handling, vague marketplace requests, or personal one-off testing.

Lead quality rule

Count this as a lead only when there is a reply email and a real request with endpoint, volume, production context, commercial use, deadline, and willingness-to-pay signal.
